Our Story

Born from the
Spirit of Mysuru

MySuru Brew started as a simple idea — bring world-class specialty coffee to the culturally rich city of Mysuru. We source beans from the Coorg and Chikmagalur estates, roast them in small batches, and brew each cup with intention.

Beyond coffee, we celebrate local flavors. Our menu weaves Mysurean culinary tradition with modern café culture — a place to slow down, connect, and savour.
